What does this mean?

A calibration curve plots the AI's predicted probabilities (e.g., 70% win chance) against the actual proportion of wins historically achieved (e.g., did 70% of those deals close won?).

Close alignment to the diagonal dashed line indicates that the model's probabilities represent actual statistical frequencies, which is vital for trust, forecasting, and pipeline scoring.
