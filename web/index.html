Model Methodology & Analysis Pipeline

This section provides a detailed view of the end-to-end data analysis, pipeline orchestration, chronological cross-validation folds, and algorithm comparisons used to build the champion predictive engine.

End-to-End Modeling Pipeline
Click any step card to inspect details
1
Historical Data Load

Loads 48 monthly snapshots of Salesforce opportunity history. Maps dynamic records across monthly periods to track stage movements and deal characteristics.

2
Leakage Protections

Excludes future closed variables (e.g. Closed Date, IsWon) from features. Enforces absolute temporal boundaries to guarantee zero target look-ahead leakage.

3
Feature Engineering

Computes 24 dynamic indicators including stage velocity, snapshot appearance frequency, slippage trackers, and cumulative client win/loss histories prior to date S.

4
Walk-Forward Folds

Splits trainable data chronologically into 3 folds. Uses sliding-window test datasets representing successive 6-month horizons to simulate real-world inference.

5
Multi-Model Training

Trains and compares 5 ML models (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, CatBoost, LightGBM, XGBoost) on outcome classifier and regression models.

6
Champion Inference & SHAP

Selects final XGBoost champion (Validation ROC AUC: 0.8372). Runs SHAP explainer to calculate local feature contribution drivers for every scored opportunity.

Explainability Framework (SHAP Values)

To drive adoption among sales partners and executive leaders, the Opportunity Intelligence Platform incorporates game-theory-based SHAP (Shapley Additive exPlanations) values.

Instead of simple feature correlation coefficients (which describe population-level trends but fail on individual deals), SHAP calculates the exact impact of each feature attribute on the win probability deviation from the global baseline.

  • Baseline Win Rate: ~31% globally.
  • Positive SHAP Drivers: Pushes deal win probability upwards (e.g., strong historical client win rate, large weighted value).
  • Negative SHAP Drivers: Pulls deal win probability downwards (e.g., stuck in current stage, frequent slippages).
Key Feature Highlights
Opp Age Days: Captures time elapsed since opportunity inception.
Days in Stage: Indicates stalls or deal stickiness.
Client Win Rate: Cumulative win ratios calculated strictly prior to the snapshot date.
Slippage Counter: Count of expected start date changes.
